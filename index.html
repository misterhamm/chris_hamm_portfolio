Who Is This Guy?

I got my degree in music education in 2006. Over the past nine years I have directed musicals, taught voice lessons, and managed about $10 million dollars in sales for an artisan bread company. After becoming disenchanted with jobs that were personally unfilling and taking me away from my family I decided to turn my hobby into a career. Becoming a developer means the opportunity to embrace my creative roots while integrating my technological passions. It is a journey I am excited to be on.

It'll be a wild ride. Join me, won't you?
